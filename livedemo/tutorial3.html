Accessing a Webserver (3/6)

Use the zoom-out botton in the toolbar to go back to the network view and identify the IP address of the www-server on your network.

Enter this IP into a web browser (using a different window from the Clack Applet page) and see the HTTP request and reply flow through your network.

Now click on a link at the bottom of this webpage to download a file to your computer and zoom back into your router to see packets being forwarded to and from the webserver.
