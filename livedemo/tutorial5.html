Analyzing Queue Occupancy (5/6)

Currently, TCP traffic is flowing through the router buffer and the ''TCP Saw-tooth'' shape is a result of TCP's congestion control. We now want to explore what happens when TCP and UDP interact.

Recall your webserver IP address. Open up Quicktime and choose File -> Open URL. Enter in the url
rtsp:///ip of webserver/sample_100kbit.mov
and press OK. Observe the interaction between the UDP flow and the router.
